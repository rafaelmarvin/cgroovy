The Evolution of C'Groovy's Website

C'Groovy, a renowned music streaming service founded in 2016, expanded its digital presence in 2024 by launching an official corporate website. Designed to strengthen brand identity and enhance user engagement, the website was developed with a focus on responsiveness, interactivity, and visual appeal. A web designer from Bina Nusantara University was entrusted with its creation, incorporating modern features such as CSS drop-down navigation, jQuery-powered recommendation sliders, and a categorized music library. The site also included a registration form with JavaScript validation, ensuring a seamless experience across devices, especially on screens under 800px wide. The website consists of five core pages: Home (showcasing UVP and top hits), About Us (company history and team profiles), Music Collection (genre-based song listings), Song Details (complete track info with lyrics and audio), and Register (a validated sign-up form).
